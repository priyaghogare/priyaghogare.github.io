Priya Ghogare

Commercial Photographer

Composing Concepts with
LENS and LIGHT.

Being a mother I know how a smile on your child's face make your day Joyous, that smile, that innocent mischievous smile on Aaryan's face is what inspired me to start child photography. And now I try my best to make sure that every mother will have a chance to cherish the memory of her child's smile for lifetime.

Wedding, probably the happiest memories of your life, where your cheeks ache because you can't stop blushing for whole day. The start of a new chemistry between the couple, the new journey together, are the moments you like to go back and visit again and again. Belive me, I have been there. So I make sure that I will freeze the best of those moments in time with my lens.

But don't let these emotional dialogs let you fool into believing that I am bounded by the metaphorical and the literal walls. I just love to connect with nature, go on the adventures and experiment with light and lens. So whatever be the project, just call me once. it's free to communicate, anyways.
